- Annual ER Visits: 450,000
- Average ER Wait Time: 3.2 hours

- Patient Satisfaction (ER): 3.1/5

- Travel Nurse Spending: $12M/year
- Malpractice Insurance: $8M/year
- IT Budget: $15M/year

AI TRIAGE VENDORS EVALUATED
1. Viz.ai — FDA-cleared, focuses on stroke/PE detection, $2.5M/year for 12 sites
2. Aidoc — Radiology AI, FDA-cleared for multiple conditions, $3.2M/year
3. TriageLogic — Telephone triage AI, not FDA-cleared for ER use, $800K/year

PROJECTED BENEFITS (AI TRIAGE)
- Wait time reduction: 35-45% (to ~1.8 hours)
- Nurse time saved: 12 minutes per triage encounter
- Estimated annual savings: $4.2M (reduced overtime + travel nurses)
- Improved ESI accuracy: from 72% to 89% (based on clinical trials)
- Patient satisfaction improvement: projected +0.8 points

REGULATORY & LEGAL CONCERNS
- FDA classification: Software as Medical Device (SaMD)
- HIPAA compliance required for all patient data
- State medical practice acts: AI cannot "practice medicine"
- Malpractice liability: unclear if AI vendor or hospital is liable
- Recent case: AI misdiagnosis lawsuit in Texas (2025) — settled $4.2M
- AMA position: AI should augment, not replace clinical judgment

IMPLEMENTATION TIMELINE
- Pilot (2 hospitals): 6 months
- Full rollout: 12-18 months
- Staff training: 80 hours per nurse
- Total 3-year cost: $18-22M
