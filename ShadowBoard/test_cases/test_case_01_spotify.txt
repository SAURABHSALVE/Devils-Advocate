SPOTIFY — PODCAST ACQUISITION ANALYSIS

Company: Spotify Technology S.A.
Ticker: SPOT (NYSE)
Report Date: Q4 2024

FINANCIAL SUMMARY
- Total Revenue: $15.7 Billion (FY 2024)
- Gross Margin: 29.2%
- Free Cash Flow: $1.4B
- Cash & Equivalents: $4.2B
- Total Debt: $1.5B

PODCAST DIVISION METRICS
- Podcast Monthly Active Users: 180M (36% of total MAU)
- Podcast Ad Revenue: $1.8B (11.5% of total revenue)
- YoY Podcast Ad Growth: 7-8%
- Exclusive Podcast Spend (2019-2024): ~$1B total
- Previous Acquisitions: Gimlet Media ($230M), Anchor ($140M), Megaphone ($235M), Podsights, Chartable

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
- Apple Podcasts: 28% market share (declining)
- YouTube Podcasts: 25% market share (fastest growing)
- Amazon Music/Audible: 12% market share
- Spotify: 30% market share (stable)
- iHeartRadio: 5% market share

PODCAST ANALYTICS MARKET
- Total addressable market: $2.8B by 2027
- Key players: Podtrac, Chartable (acquired by Spotify), Edison Research, Triton Digital
- Gap: Real-time cross-platform attribution analytics
- Estimated acquisition target valuation: $150M - $400M

RISKS
- Podcast ROI has been questioned by investors since 2022
- Joe Rogan deal ($250M) showed mixed retention impact
- Shift from exclusives to open ecosystem in 2023
- YouTube emerging as dominant video podcast platform
